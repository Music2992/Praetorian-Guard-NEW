Our Story

Clan Description

When the Nerotonin outbreak tore through Fairview City, the world as people knew it, ended overnight. The streets fell silent under the shadow of death, then erupted with screams and chaos. From that chaos rose warlords who thrived on fear, carving out power from the desperation of survivors. Among them was a man known only as "Unknown", a figure who ruled through intimidation, bending others to his will and calling it protection.

But there were those who refused to bow. Four friends, XSoulreaperX1, Perminite, J A M E S B O N D, and Labyss, challenged him head-on. In a decisive act, they stripped him of power, broke his hold over the helpless, and ended his reign and tyranny. From that victory, the Praetorian Guard emerged once more, reborn, not as an army of servants, but as a disciplined brotherhood bound by honor and equality.

The Sovereign Domain: The group takes a job to secure a seemingly worthless piece of land—a derelict space station, an abandoned luxury bunker, a forgotten island. They soon discover it's the last truly safe haven in a collapsing world. Now, they are no longer just its guards; they are its founders, its lawmakers, its first citizens. They must build a society from scratch, deciding together what kind of "family" they will be.

The Praetorian Guard did not seek dominion. Their purpose was the protection of their own. Each member lived by the principles of loyalty, unity, and unwavering resolve. In the clan, discipline was not merely a rule, it was the difference between life and death. Survival was a duty shared by all.

Through the ruins of Fairview, they stood their ground against the infected and against the cruelty of man alike. Their strength was shaped by the many who fought beside them: Miguel08, Skyflakes15, Papalate, Draco (Feza), Yuyo, Alcoholic Fish, LuzZiomek, SethGreedy, Crispy Lizard, D1zzy, Rishu, Exslayer (Quing Ling), and Preterians. Some had fallen, others had vanished, but all remained part of the Guard's living legacy.

In a world drowning in death, the Praetorian Guard stood as more than survivors. They were guardians of each other, disciplined, united, and unbroken.
